Lightsprint for better world
Lightsprint does not just illuminate, globally, in realtime. The ultimate goal is making world a better place.

10% to charity now

Lightsprint donates 10% of its revenue to charity and non-profits advancing knowledge, reason, freedom and transparency.

No patents

Lightsprint does not patent novel techniques employed in Lightsprint SDK. Although software patents generate profit for inventors, they slow down progress for everyone else.

100% to charity in future

One day in far future, Lightsprint will donate everything and open source all code.
