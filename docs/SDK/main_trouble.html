Troubleshooting

Please report us problems you encounter. Your feedback is very important.

  • Not enough memory?
  • Windows executables don't run
  • Wrong color bleeding in Gamebryo
  • Wrong up direction, scene is 90 degree rotated
  • Wrong units, scene too small or too large

Known GPU driver bugs (with the most recent drivers):

  • Nvidia
    • When repeatedly changing resolution of reflection cubemap by one, artifacts shows up in reflection (driver seems to interpret mipmapped cube as not mipmapped).
      Workaround: don't change cubemap resolution by one.
  • Intel
    • sRGB correct rendering does not work, scene is darker in sRGB mode.
      Workaround: render without "sRGB correct" option.
